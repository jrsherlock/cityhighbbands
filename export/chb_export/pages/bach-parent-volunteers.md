To all band families at The School That Leads…

 

On behalf of the Band Associates of City High (BACH), I’m
excited to welcome each and every one of you to the start of what is sure to be another incredible year of making music and memories at City High.  I would specifically like to welcome all families who are new additions to our City High Band family this year. We are excited to meet you and
welcome you to our City High Band family!  For those of you who are new to City High Band or are unfamiliar with our BACH organization, our mission and core values are as follows:

 

***We are parents and
community members** who volunteer our time and talent in support of the City High Band program.*

***We are here to support
the City High band directors and students**.*

***We are
inclusionary** and believe that **every student at City High** should have the chance to benefit from the opportunities that the band program provides.*

***Our goal** is to ensure that the City High Band program has the
resources it needs to offer the highest quality educational and extracurricular opportunities for band students. We provide support through fundraising, providing volunteers, and planning
extracurricular events at no cost to students.*
